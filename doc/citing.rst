Citing pystella
===============

    P. Adshead, J. T. Giblin, M. Pieroni, and Z. J. Weiner,
    Phys. Rev. Lett. 124 (2020) 171301, arXiv:1909.12843 [astro-ph.CO].

    P. Adshead, J. T. Giblin, M. Pieroni, and Z. J. Weiner,
    Phys. Rev. D101 (2020) 083534, arXiv:1909.12842 [astro-ph.CO].
